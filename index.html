Location

The most popular option is to meet you in your favourite local park. We can also provide our parties in your house/garden or you can hire a local hall.

What activities can we expect?

We have a number of incredibly fun partner and group activities including: Dodgeball, Zombie Tag, Wacky Relay Races, Scavenger Hunt, Obstacle Course, Football, Ultimate Frisbee, Tug of War and much much more!

How does it work?

We start by coming to visit your school, with the aim of finding out what you are looking to achieve. We can show you testimonials, and some extensive examples of our work with other schools. Once we have an idea of what you are looking to achieve, we put together a a few programmes that hit all of your targets. You then get to review this and try us out for a free session.

Personal Training

Our trainers provide varied, specific workouts geared towards individual goals to ensure each participant reaches them. Sessions are delivered at a place and time suitable to you.

What does Our Training include?

Muki Sports Personal Training includes nutritional and lifestyle advice, ongoing motivational support and goal tracking. We are also willing to recommend follow up exercises during breaks.

Who are Fitness Boot Camps for?
